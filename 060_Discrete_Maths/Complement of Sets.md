# Complement of Sets

## Complement of [[Set]]s

If U is the universal set containing everything, then the complement of a [[Subset]] A, will be everything not in set A, and is denoted $\bar{A}$.

$$
\bar{A} = \{ x \in U | x \not\in A \}
$$

Or

$$
\bar{A}= U\setminus A
$$
